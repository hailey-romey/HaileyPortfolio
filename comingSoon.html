I believe an inspired project is engaging beyond a solution, it innovates new interactions and serves to inspire its audience. Ever since getting my first digital camera (a Nikon coolpix, complete with 8 whole megapixels!) I've been obsessed with telling stories through digital media. From sibling-acted stories to high school film sets and eventually to sketching storyboards and app flows, I have always enjoyed crafting memorable, human-centric experiences. I recently completed my Bachelor of Science in Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University, and I'm currently working to improve my compositional skills in both music and visual design.
